Nano Banana

free image generation, built into Google Gemini

pros & cons

the good stuff

  • completely free
  • no extra account needed
  • fast and easy to access
  • great for playful experimentation

worth knowing

  • less control over fine details
  • outputs can be unpredictable
  • better for ideas than precision

what people make with it

  • illustrated concepts and visual notes
  • whimsical scene generation
  • visual brainstorming and mood boards
  • quick reference images for projects

get started

  1. Open Google Gemini and sign in with your Google account
  2. Type an image description — try something like "a banana reading a book in soft morning light"
  3. Nano Banana will generate it right in the chat
  4. Save what you like, ignore what you don't
